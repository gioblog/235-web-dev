Notes

For the site, I wanted to keep the designs and the text simplistic to try and achieve a professional, news-like look to the web page. This is why most of the font is Merriweather with the default being any type of sans-serif font. Since this is an accurate portrayal of a media outlet within my dystopian world, it is assumed that the audience already knows how the world functions. None of the basic parts of my world is explained, but I tried to add enough context clues that you could piece togetehr a bit of what the world is like. I had the most trouble with making the navigation bar hidden in the home icon. The animated robot is my own drawing and I modeled the mask depicted.
