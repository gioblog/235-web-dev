Sugar Rush

High Concept

As a server, you need to serve food to the correct customer orders. Make a mistake or make the customer wait too long and the cafe will lose money!

Genre

Job Simulation, Casual, Point and Click

Aesthetics

I'm going for a cutesy style with a limited color palette. The main colors will be pastel purple and pastle yellow.

Story

There isn't much of a story. You are just a recent hire at this cafe, Iets Zoets.

Platform

The game will only be playable using a desktop on the web.

Gameplay

Customers will appear at the window with a text bubble that shows what food item they want to order.

Mockups

Stretch Goals

To add more to the gameplay, I'd want the players to "craft" the orders with a few ingredients and a Minecraft crafting grid.
